D. Vivid Teal + Rose

Higher saturation teal (hue 190°) — distinctly blue-teal, vibrant but not electric. Paired with a true rose/pink accent (hue 10°) instead of orange-coral. More contemporary, slightly bolder.

AAA Primary text on white: 9.8:1
AA Button text on teal-600: 5.0:1

💡 Key differences: A (192°) and B (195°) are the most distinctly teal — B is calmer. C (200°) is the most muted/sophisticated — almost gray. D (190°) is the most vibrant. The accent colors range from terracotta (A) to peach-coral (B) to amber (C) to rose (D). All pass WCAG AA minimum; B and C achieve AAA on buttons too.

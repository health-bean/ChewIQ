Final design system:
• Brand: Teal (hue 195°) — only chromatic color
• Neutrals: Warm-tinted (hue 80°) — cream surface, warm grays
• Logo: "Chew" in teal-800, "IQ" in teal-500 italic
• Semantic: Green (allowed), red (symptoms/avoid), amber (warnings), purple (supplements) — from data, not brand
• Display: Fraunces (serif) for headings and logo
• Body: Source Sans 3 for everything else
• Components: Hybrid soft-layer — ghost border + warm shadow

A. Botanical Clinical

Warm sage + coral. Organic serif headings (Fraunces). Feels like a high-end wellness clinic — precise but human. Cream backgrounds, warm shadows.

Pros

  • Warm and trustworthy
  • Already partially implemented
  • Strong brand differentiation

Cons

  • Serif headings may feel "old" to some
  • Sage can read dull if not balanced with coral

B. Soft Modern

Lavender + rose. All sans-serif (DM Sans). Feels like a modern wellness app — Calm meets Headspace. Pure white cards, gentle purple tints.

Pros

  • Approachable and modern
  • Familiar wellness-app feel
  • Easier to maintain (one font)

Cons

  • Risks looking like every other health app
  • Purple is overused in wellness space

C. Warm Earth

Walnut brown + terracotta. Serif + sans-serif mix. Feels like a leather journal — grounded, analog, timeless. Parchment textures, earthy tones.

Pros

  • Most distinctive and memorable
  • Strong "healing journey" narrative
  • Unique in the health app space

Cons

  • Brown can feel dated if not executed well
  • Needs careful contrast for accessibility

💡 My recommendation: Option A (Botanical Clinical) — it's the strongest balance of warmth, professionalism, and brand distinctiveness. The sage/coral palette is unique in the health app space, and the serif display font adds character without sacrificing readability. We've already started this direction.

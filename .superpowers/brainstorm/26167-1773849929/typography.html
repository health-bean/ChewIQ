B. Source Sans 3

C. Outfit

Geometric with rounded terminals — friendly and modern. Slightly larger visual weight makes it very readable for fatigued users. Has a gentle, approachable personality.

💡 My recommendation: Option A (DM Sans) — it's already implemented, pairs well with Fraunces, and has the best balance of warmth and readability. But compare the small text carefully — look at the badge text, navigation, and body paragraphs. Option C (Outfit) is a strong alternative if you want something softer.

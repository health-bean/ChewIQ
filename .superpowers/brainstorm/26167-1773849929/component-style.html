B. Outlined & Structured

C. Hybrid — Soft Layers

Subtle shadow + ghost border. Cards have just enough lift to feel tactile without floating away. Inputs are bordered, buttons have slight depth. The warmest, most layered feel — like paper stacked on a desk.

💡 My recommendation: Option C (Hybrid). It strikes the "Botanical Clinical" balance perfectly — the thin ghost borders add clinical structure while the warm shadows add organic depth. A is too floaty for a data-heavy health app. B is too stark for a healing-journey product.
